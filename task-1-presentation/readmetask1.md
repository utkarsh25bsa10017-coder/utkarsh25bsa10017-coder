
The Tool I Used:
For this assignment, I utilized Canva to design my digital literacy awareness infographic. The platform provided an intuitive interface with a vast library of graphic elements, making it ideal for visual storytelling.

What My Design Shows:
My design, titled "Digital Literacy Essentials," is structured as a quick-reference guide for my batchmates. It covers five key areas: defining digital literacy, highlighting useful digital tools, outlining safe internet practices, explaining proper email etiquette, and building a professional online presence. I organized the information using distinct, color-coded sections and relevant iconography so each topic is easily digestible.

An Interesting Challenge:
One interesting challenge while making this infographic was visually representing abstract concepts like cybersecurity. I had to search specifically for "data privacy" graphics to find the right padlock and shield icons that clearly conveyed "Safe Internet Practices" without relying on extensive text. Balancing these visuals with concise, actionable bullet points was a valuable exercise in effective communication.
